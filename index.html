Important Notice

Author's message

- As a fan of Shad's and Jazza's simple and realistic approach to roleplaying, I'm a bit concerned by the realistic aspect getting lost since they handed it over to the community. Quite frankly: While I have no problem sharing stuff, I don't trust public voting to make the right decisions. Cogent is not mine to manage, I have 1 vote of many, and even if the comminity would accept all of my suggestions, the adoption process is far too slow for me, so I started Ultra Cogent (my homebrew) with even more realism in mind. :)
- Ultra Cogent has substantial changes, mostly my own ideas, but also some adopted ideas.

Contribution

- You may use it, share it, fork it and/or make suggestions, but keep in mind that Ultra Cogent is mine and thus I may or may not accept your input.

- I will ignore any request to include woke ideologies!
    - I've seen games granting me highly destructive weapons, only to educate me about gun control. - Screw that!
    - I've seen games full of feminism, inclisivity, gender ideologies etc. - I hear that a lot, I ignore that a lot, it annoys me every time and breaks the immersion, so it's time for me to push back against it!
None of those sound fun to me, so if that's what you want to suggest, I suggest you bugger off and make your own fork! :P

Navigation tips

- Hover the mouse to the left, or tap the arrow at the top left corner to reveal the menu.
- Click/tap the Sun/Moon in the top right corner to switch between light and dark themes.

Cookies

- This site may use cookies for character data, although it's a static github page, which means no data is sent back to the server.
(That being said your data is as safe as your browser allows, thus your character data may be lost on exit if your browser is set to discard cookies, or if you choose to delete cookies.)
