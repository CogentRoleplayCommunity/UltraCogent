My Vision for Ultra Cogent

What is it?!

        It's an ultra realistic universal table top roleplay system. A set of unbalanced rules and game mechanics based on Cogent Roleplay but fully embracing realism over fairness. (Life just isn't fair and genetics is a lottery... Deal with it! :P)
More of a framework that allows you to build a game easy and fast rather then a ready to play game, although some content may be provided.

Embracing Reality

        Thinking and discussing skills with other cogent community devs, trying to come up with a good set of skills that make sense and balance in a game of any theme, it became evident that there are far more intelligence based skills then strenght or reflex based. No wonder... Big brain allows us humans to dominate raw strength and reflex of lesser beings. We have developped tactics for hunting and we have creative tools and techniques for survival. That's an advantage that less intelligent beings just don't have.
        This makes it impossible to come up with a set of skills that is both balanced and realistic. Most people think a game should be fair(aka balanced). I tend to value realism more, because it makes the game more immersive, although it requires adults at the table who are there for the advanture however challenging it is, rather then for the win and thus capable of cheering for the one who does most of the fighting, or most of the problem solving! It also allows for use of the game mechanic as a writing aid, which is also one of my goals. (I'm also an aspiring writer, and I will make it solo playable for that exact reason.)
        I think of TTRPG as more of an adult's game, not really for children, so I chose to target an adult player base, and I'm not going to treat my target audience as children incapable enjoying an inherently unbalanced game. If unfair is a deal breaker for you, there's the Cogent Community Project, and most other TTRPG games which are going in the balanced direction. (Alternatively you can fork this project and make a balanced version.)
        I also concluded that much of the skills are based on more then one attribute. Shad and Jazza recognized this, but only in regard to Cogent's combat mechanic. Ultra Cogent however will have a primary + optional secondary and even tertiary attribute bonus(es) / malus(es) to each skill check.
